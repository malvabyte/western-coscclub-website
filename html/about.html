About:

Welcome to the Filesmithing Club at Western Wyoming Community College! We are a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, offering a space to learn, collaborate, and explore the world of Computer Science, including programming, networking, ethical hacking, and single-board computers.

Whether you're a seasoned coder or a beginner, our weekly meetings, enthusiastic executive board, and supportive advisors, Jennifer Allen and Dr. Tammy Robertson, provide a welcoming environment to expand your tech horizons. As a member, you'll have access to valuable resources, the opportunity to shape our club's direction, and the chance to participate in fascinating tech projects. Join us in the exciting journey of innovation, problem-solving, and discovery. Connect with us today, and let's code the future together!
